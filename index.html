About Us

Meet The Engineer

My name is Dillon Loucks and I have been a performing musician for 10 years, releasing several EPs across multiple projects. I've spent that time learning the tools necessary to write and produce a well written song. I understand how to take music from the drawing board to a full fledged production. I have worked with many artists since I began my journey including, Midwinter, Slight Break, and even a song for wrestler Ryder Reid. My goal is to bring you audio production that exceeds your expectations and entices your audience.
